What is Tower of Hanoi?

Tower of Hanoi is a simple puzzle game where you attempt to move a stack of increasingly large disks from one peg to another. The catch is that you can only move one disk at a time and you cannot put a larger disk on top of a smaller disk (i.e., the disks must always be ordered). There are three pegs that you can place disks on: one where your tower starts, one that is empty, and one where you are attempting to move the tower to.
